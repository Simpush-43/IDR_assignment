About IDR

Industry-Led.
Impact-Driven.

The Institute of Digital Risk is an industry-led training and deployment institute purpose-built for digital, cyber, and technology risk. We exist to close the widening gap between academic knowledge and the practical skills demanded by regulated, high-consequence environments — from financial services to critical national infrastructure.

Through deep partnerships with leading UK universities and private sector organisations, IDR develops and deploys risk practitioners who are technically fluent, commercially aware, and ready to operate from day one. Our programmes blend rigorous academic frameworks with live project experience, producing talent that doesn't just understand risk — it manages it.

Our Model
🎓 Academic Partnership

Collaborating with leading UK universities to embed research-grade thinking into every programme — from threat modelling to AI governance.

🏭 Industry Practice

Every IDR cohort works on real projects with real organisations. No simulations. No hypotheticals. Real risk, real consequence.

🚀 Deployment Ready

Graduates are placed directly into roles across financial services, defence, and critical infrastructure — assessed and vouched for by IDR.
